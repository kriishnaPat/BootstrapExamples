Containers

The container class changes how much of the viewport content covers!

  • "container": max-width (a little bit less than a 100%) at each breakpoint; 100% at less than extra small!
  • "container-sm": 100% of viewport until SM breakpoint
  • "container-md": 100% of viewport until MD breakpoint
  • "container-lg": 100% of viewport until LG breakpoint
  • "container-xl": 100% of viewport until XL breakpoint
  • "container-xxl": 100% of viewport until XXL breakpoint
  • "container-fluid": 100% of viewport always
